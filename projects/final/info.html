Product Information

Our products are made from US highway billboard vinyl, each wallet is a one-of-a-kind, extremely durable, thin, and downright awesome. (RFID protected).

The Palette

These are the colors you get to choose from when you get a wallet. (Once the campaign is successful, we'll contact you and find out what color(s) you want!)

Surf and ski bag rewards are made out of the same durable, waterproof billboard vinyl material as the wallets. Becasue of the size of these, details will come through and they'll have a brilliant amount of character. Each one of these is custom designed by us, so we'll take down your favorite color(s) and find them on a good lookin part of the billboard. And remember, these wallets are made from high quality U.S. sourced upcycled billboard vinyl and made right here in Minnesota by high end top outdoor brand sewers. The prices on here are a one time special for Kickstarters as a huge thanks to helping us get this off the ground!
