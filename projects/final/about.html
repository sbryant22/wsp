About EpicStoke

EpicStoke was founded by two brothers, Joe and Nick Schaeppi, from Maple Grove, MN.

The Brief Story

Hey Kickstarters! So the story goes... we were in search of the perfect way to make the perfect wallet. Basically a wallet that was:

  • versatile yet fits your precise needs
  • enjoyable to use, thin, durable, & protects valuables from the elements
  • can be broken in like leather but won't get wet like it
  • one of a kind, has great design, and tells a story
  • Oh, and is locally made from high quality recycled or sustainable resources

Impossible? Almost, but not quite.

We realized that there was one main ingredient aside from great design necessary to fulfill all those requirements: a perfect material- billboard vinyl. It has everything we ever hoped and wished for. And aside from being a designer's dream, by purchasing a wallet you are saving something that doesn't like to break down from going into landfills or being burned into the air.

We just want to sincerely thank all of you in advance for taking the time to read this and for those of you who decide to support this in any way, you rock.

Please help share this story with your friends on facebook, twitter, email or your preferred sharing mechanism, whatever it may be- we really appreciate it!
